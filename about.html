About Cane & Camera

Cane & Camera is a wildlife and nature storytelling project from Rajasthan — blending documentary filmmaking with fine art stills. From Mukundara Hills Tiger Reserve to the Thar's fragile grasslands, these images are made with patience, respect and deep local ties.

This site showcases selected works available as limited edition prints and licensed digital downloads.
